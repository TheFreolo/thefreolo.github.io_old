Calcolo delle probabilità e statistica matematica

The following links provide material for the course I am teaching assisting that is called Calcolo delle probabilità e statistica matematica. In the part of the course covered by these pages, I introduce R to the students and I show some applications to statistics.

The course introduces the fundamental concepts related to probability and statistics. It is part of the bachelor degree in Mathematics at the University of Trento (Italy).

Biostatistics

The aim of the course in Biostatistics is to provide the students, especially those with bachelor degrees in biology-related subjects, with the tools and the understanding to properly use statistics for analyzing and drawing exhaustive conclusions from biological data.

During the course, the teacher introduces many concepts of mathematics, statistics, probability and machine learning, and, at the same time, these concepts are linked to biology-related case studies.

This course is part of the master degree in Quantitative and Computational Biology at the University of Trento (Italy).
